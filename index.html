We came together at the digital borders of the sky, supporting each other and pursuing our love, and formed the 110 Squadron Squadron. Dec. The abilities of each of you in the virtual skies offer not only a game, but also an experience full of passion and love of the weather.


We, united in digital cockpits, fly high with the determination and courage of real-world pilots. Our team spirit allows us to create a real connection in the digital world by combining the love of aviation of each of us. Every moment we spend together contributes to us becoming stronger in virtual battles in the sky.


110 Hür Squadron is not only a game team, but also a family. The passion and knowledge of each of you make our quadron a real force. The dedication of each individual in the team and the love of the weather unites us even more.


Each flight adds a new layer to our memories of our time together. The excitement of working together to achieve our goals goes beyond the real world. Our digital struggles in the sky come to life not only beyond the screen, but also in our hearts.


110 Hür Squadron, as a family gathered together in the digital skies, we will continue to strengthen our bond with each other and further glorify our love of aviation. Dec. Together, let's keep pushing the boundaries of the sky!
